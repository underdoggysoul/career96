Human-Centered Research

After extensive research, we created an e-survey to determine the public’s view regarding the topic. Results showed that people are unaware of the SmartNation Campaign despite the government’s efforts.

Out of the 5 main factors SmartNation hopes to achieve , our respondents ranked “turning textbook learning into real life experiences” second as the most important factor with 69% of the votes.

Creative Ideation

Based on the information that we have gathered, we saw a market yearning for knowledge hence we formulated 2 solutions.

They are ‘Online E-learning’ and ‘Job Internship’. After we deliberated on the feasibility of both solutions we decided to create a Career Guidance portal.

We intend to create an opportunity for students to discover real life jobs first hand through tours and internships. This is because research has proven that individuals that realise their dreams have more passion and excitement in their current job.

Hence, we hope to develop student’s interest for them to sense a greater purpose to excel in academics and other platforms. Moreover, we also hope to provide an opportunity for students to exercise their creativity through real life experiences.

Iterative Prototype

We will be collaborating with schools to help the students so that it will be more efficient. Schools that want to participate can contact us through email to work with our staff team.

After much consideration, we decided that starting off with a website would help in promoting our services and allow keen students to sign up.

Students can register for field trips, tours and internships through the website to better get in touch with their job interests.

Evaluation

Milestones will allow students to keep track of how much they have progressed so far and achievements.We are going to nurture the students from a young age and help them find their passion.

Therefore, we plan to have these services, exhibition, competition, tours and internship to encourage students to participate more actively and learn to work their way towards their ambition. Therefore, we are confident that the project will work out.
